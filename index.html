About Placemate

Placemate is an integrated platform created to simplify the entire placement preparation process for students. We understand that preparing for placements can be overwhelming — managing aptitude, reasoning, technical knowledge, and interview readiness all at once. Placemate solves this by providing everything in one structured place.

Our goal is to make placement preparation smarter, not harder. With real-time progress tracking, AI-based performance analysis, and regularly updated question banks from top recruiters, students get the most accurate and effective preparation experience.

Placemate isn’t just a website — it’s your study companion, tracking your growth and helping you achieve your dream job through consistency and guided learning.
